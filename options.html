How it works

Built-in sites — GitHub, GitLab, Jira / Confluence, dev.to, Stack Overflow, Linear, Hashnode, and the major LLM chats — work automatically. On any other site, click the Beauty Diagram toolbar icon and turn on Beautify on this site. Already-rendered diagrams get replaced; plain code blocks show a ◇ Render button you click to beautify.
